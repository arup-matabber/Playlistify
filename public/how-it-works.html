Step-by-Step Process

01

Describe Your Vibe

Type in natural language what kind of music you're in the mood for. Whether it's "rainy day melancholy" or "खुश बॉलीवुड पार्टी" - we understand it all!

English हिंदी தமிழ் తెలుగు
02

AI Analyzes Your Intent

Our NLP engine parses your description to understand mood, genre preferences, energy level, and emotional undertones. It maps keywords to Spotify's genre seeds.

Mood Detection Genre Mapping Keyword Analysis
03

Spotify API Magic

We query Spotify's extensive catalog of 100M+ songs using smart search algorithms and recommendation seeds to find tracks that match your vibe perfectly.

100M+ Tracks Smart Search Recommendations
04

Playlist Created!

A brand new playlist is created in Spotify with 20 perfectly curated tracks. Click "Open in Spotify" to start listening immediately!

20 Tracks Saved to Spotify Instant Access
